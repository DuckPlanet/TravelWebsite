ECONOMIC STATE OF ILOILO

- The economy of Iloilo province and this highly urbanised city have posted positive growth in 2021 at 4.5 percent and 6.8 percent, respectively, a big rebound compared to their performance in 2020 which was at -6.0 percent for the province and -12.8 percent for the city.

The service industry contributed 3.1 points. Agriculture, forestry and fisheries scored 1.1 points. and industry rose 0.4 percentage points against his 4.5% economic growth in the state.

Iloilo City's gross city domestic product (GCDP) was valued at PHP132.4 billion,rise from PHP123.9 billion in 2020. In 2021, the city of Iloilo had the highest per capita GPDP/GCDP of PHP 280,356 among the eight Western Visayas economies. The average contribution of each individual to the city's economy is expressed as GPDP per capita.

Source: https://www.pna.gov.ph/articles/1190696
