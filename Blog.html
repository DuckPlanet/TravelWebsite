This 7.8-hectare property is a gem on its own, with gorgeous villas, rooms, and glamping tents offering top-notch comfort and relaxation. Beyond servicing its guests and promoting the beauty of the Gigantes, Solina also helps the community by providing job opportunities to the locals. 85% of its employees are purely Carlesanons who offer remarkable hospitality and service to the resortâ€™s guests.
